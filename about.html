About PowerDataX

Founded in 2015, PowerDataX provides secure, enterprise-grade data infrastructure for organizations where reliability, security, and compliance are non-negotiable requirements.

Our Mission

To enable organizations to leverage their data assets securely and responsibly, providing the infrastructure needed to drive informed decision-making while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and compliance.

What We Do

PowerDataX designs, implements, and maintains secure data platforms for government agencies, healthcare systems, and large enterprises. Our solutions enable these organizations to consolidate, analyze, and govern their data assets while meeting stringent regulatory requirements.

We specialize in environments where data sensitivity requires enhanced security measures, including multi-layered encryption, comprehensive audit trails, and strict access controls.

Zero-Trust Security

Built on a zero-trust security model where no entity is trusted by default. Every access request is verified, every data movement is logged.

Privacy by Design

Privacy protections are embedded into our architecture from the ground up, ensuring compliance with global data protection regulations.

Our Journey

2015

Foundation

PowerDataX founded by former government and enterprise security architects. Initial focus on secure data federation for federal agencies.

2017

Healthcare Expansion

Launched HIPAA-compliant platform for healthcare systems. Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification for security controls.

2019

Enterprise Adoption

Expanded to Fortune 500 enterprise clients. Implemented FedRAMP Moderate authorization for government cloud services.

2021

Global Expansion

Established data centers in EU and APAC regions. Achieved ISO 27001 certification for information security management.

2023

Current Position

Serving 250+ enterprise clients across 40+ countries. Maintaining 99.99% platform availability with zero security breaches.
